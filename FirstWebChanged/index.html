Telcaria is the advanced network service virtualization company serving the dynamic network operators.

Nowadays, voice and Internet access are totally commoditized. Thus Telcos are forced to compete in price, resulting in sustained trend in profit margin reduction.
What actions are needed to increase profits?

  • Lower costs by combining SDN and NFV
  • Increase added value by service differentiation

That is, Telco Virtualization: from Hardware-based companies to Software-based companies.

Telcaria provides products and services for Software-Based Telcos, but we also provide Web Technology developments, Internet Services and ICT solutions integration at request. Please visit the 'Services' section for more info.

Telcaria costumers:

  • SaaS to drive developer productivity with a vendor-neutral, standard-based open-source platforms
  • Network Operators to differentiate their platform in the high-growth IaaS marketplace
  • Enterprise IT Operations to quickly scale up and support proven secure, affordable, flexible cloud resources, as well as other integrated solutions
  • Infrastructure Technology Suppliers to enable a rapid growth across the virtualization marketplace
